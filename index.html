WOLF IN SHEEP'S CLOTHING

THE BMW M5 COMPETITION EDITION PUSHING LIMITS.STRICTLY LIMITED.

Very few sedans generate as much heart beating and adrenaline as the BMW M5. Especially when it comes in a limited Special Edition with Competition Package. Cast in the visually stunning BMW Individual special paint finish: Mineral White metallic, this exceptional BMW M5 also sets the pulses racing with its elegant side.

In this Special Edition, the performance of the powerful 4.4 litre M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine has been increased to a

breathtaking 600 HP (441 kW).
A modified suspension with firmer spring and damper set-up ensures even more dynamic control. The specially adjusted ride height lowered by 10 millimetres provides more stability, both on the race track and in everyday use. The steering with M specific Servotronic Mode and directly configured characteristic curve ensure that every steering command is performed with maximum precision.

The BMW M5 unites two worlds more than any other car: ultimate motor sport feeling and everyday suitability.
The already dynamic look is boosted once again by the BMW M Performance Parts.
